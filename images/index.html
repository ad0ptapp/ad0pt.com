Executive Summary

Ad0pt was created to assist in the reduction of animal shelter populations by giving individuals an easy way to find homes for the animals that they can no longer care for. Subsequently, it also serves to assist those looking to adopt an animal by allowing the user to see a detailed profile of the animal of their interest. By providing a convenient and friendly environment Ad0ption LLC hopes to gain ground in the world of animal care.
